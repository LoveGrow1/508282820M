Action recommendations

Deepen Emotional Support:

Try to focus more on emotional support, especially during times of sadness or upset. Be proactive and ask her how she's feeling and what she needs from you.

Clarify Honesty Concerns:

Find a quiet, calm moment to bring up your concerns about honesty in your relationship. It could be that you're perceiving something that isn't there, or there might be a deeper issue that needs addressing.

Balancing Personal Space:

Being in a relationship for more than a decade and raising a child together, you both might feel the need for some personal space. Consider designating some alone time for each other, a time where you can each pursue personal interests, hobbies, or just relax.
